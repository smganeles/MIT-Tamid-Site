What is TAMID?

TAMID is a non-profit organization that develops business skills of undergraduate students. Our members gain access to a comprehensive education curriculum, pro-bono consulting for Tel Aviv-based startups, and capital market investment research.

Why TAMID?

1000 undergraduate students each year go on to work in finance and management consulting from MIT

There are not enough business-centered clubs on campus to cater to interested students

Why MIT?

MIT is a top ranked school with a motivated and hardworking student body

MIT is an innovation hub and many students & faculty go on to found their own start-ups

MIT is surrounded by established TAMID chapters at universities in the greater Boston area
